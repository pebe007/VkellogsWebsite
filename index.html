VKellog’s
Kellogg's is an American multinational food manufacturing company that produces a wide variety of cereals, snacks, and other food products. The company was founded by W.K. Kellogg in 1906 and is headquartered in Battle Creek, Michigan, USA. Kellogg's is known for its iconic brands such as Pop-Tarts, Frosted Flakes, and Special K, among many others. The company has a significant global presence, with its products manufactured and marketed in over 180 countries
